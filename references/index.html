References

On this page references are listed, such as books, articles, podcasts, links etc. which I were and still are very valuable resources to me.

Data Science Problems

Kaggle Online competitions in data science including tutorials to get started with machine learning tasks etc. There are also free datasets which can be use for your own projects

No free hunch. Kaggles official blogg including very interesting interviews, hints, tips to all kinds of data science related topics

Blogs and Email Newsletters

Data Elixir. A weekly newsletter of curated data science news and resources from around the web. Free for data lovers.
Python Weekly. A free weekly newsletter featuring curated news, articles, new releases, jobs etc related to Python.
Data Machina. A free weekly digest of data science curiosities etc.
DataTau. Hacker news for data scientists, conversations about various data science topics.
HackerNews. Conversations about various content on the web.

Podcasts

Ideal resource to get latest news and/or basic understanding in the data science area while comuting. Data Stories Podcast. A podcast on data visualization with Enrico Bertini and Moritz Stefaner.
Linear Digressions. Podcast on various data science topics, summarized in 15 to 20 minutes podcast.
