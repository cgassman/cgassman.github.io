References

On this page I want to list references, such as books, articles, links etc. that were and still are very valuable to me. They helped me a lot to get into the topic of data science, but also programming with Python.
My favorite links are currently the following ones. I really check them on a regular basis...at least weekly!...because I almost always find something new or very interesting.

CheckIO. I think this is a very cool way to learn/improve coding for example with Python. You got different coding-quests to solve. I really like this!

Kaggle One of my favorite pages! Online competitions in data science including tutorials to get started with machine learning tasks etc. You can also find free datasets on kaggle that you can use for your own projects

No free hunch. This is kaggles official blogg and you find very interesting interviews, hints, tips to all kinds of data science related topics

Blogs and Email Newsletters

Python Weekly. A free weekly newsletter featuring curated news, articles, new releases, jobs etc related to Python.
Pycoders Weekly. A free weekly email newsletter for those interested in python development and various topics around python.
Data Stories Podcast. A podcast on data visualization with Enrico Bertini and Moritz Stefaner.
Data Machina. A free weekly digest of data science curiosities etc.
Topcoder not so familiar with Topcoder yet. I've just found out about a few days ago, but will check it out in the near future.
